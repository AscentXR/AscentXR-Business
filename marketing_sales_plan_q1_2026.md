## ðŸ’¼ SALES STRATEGY

### SALES PROCESS (45-60 day cycle)

#### Stage 1: Lead Qualification (Days 1-3)
**Activities:**
- Lead scoring (0-100 points)
- Initial research (LinkedIn, website, news)
- Qualification call (15 minutes)
- CRM data entry

**Qualification Criteria (BANT):**
- **Budget:** $5K+ available for pilot
- **Authority:** Decision maker or strong influencer
- **Need:** Clear use case identified
- **Timeline:** Implementation within 6 months

**Exit Criteria:**
- Qualified lead score >60
- Need confirmed
- Next meeting scheduled

#### Stage 2: Discovery (Days 4-10)
**Activities:**
- Deep discovery call (45 minutes)
- Stakeholder mapping
- Technical requirements gathering
- Competitive landscape assessment

**Key Questions:**
1. What are your biggest curriculum challenges?
2. How do you currently teach [subject]?
3. What technology initiatives are underway?
4. Who else is involved in the decision?
5. What does success look like?
6. What's your evaluation process?

**Exit Criteria:**
- All stakeholders identified
- Technical requirements documented
- Custom demo scope defined
- Budget range confirmed

#### Stage 3: Demo & Validation (Days 11-20)
**Activities:**
- Custom demo preparation
- Live demonstration (45-60 minutes)
- Technical deep-dive (if needed)
- Trial/pilot setup (if applicable)

**Demo Structure:**
1. **Hook (5 min):** Relatable problem statement
2. **Solution (30 min):** Custom use case demonstration
3. **Proof (15 min):** Case studies and data
4. **Next Steps (10 min):** Clear path forward

**Exit Criteria:**
- Positive demo feedback
- Technical validation complete
- Proposal scope agreed
- Timeline confirmed

#### Stage 4: Proposal & Negotiation (Days 21-35)
**Activities:**
- Formal proposal creation
- Pricing presentation
- ROI documentation
- Contract negotiation

**Proposal Components:**
1. Executive summary
2. Scope of work
3. Pricing and payment terms
4. Implementation timeline
5. Success metrics
6. Case studies
7. Terms and conditions

**Pricing Strategy:**
- **Custom Experience:** $15K-$50K (one-time)
- **Subscription:** $3K-$10K/year
- **Professional Services:** $150/hour
- **Pilot Program:** $2K (credited to full contract)

**Exit Criteria:**
- Proposal delivered and reviewed
- Pricing agreed
- Contract sent

#### Stage 5: Close (Days 36-45)
**Activities:**
- Contract negotiation
- Legal review
- Signature collection
- Kickoff scheduling

**Closing Techniques:**
1. **Assumptive Close:** "When would you like to start implementation?"
2. **Alternative Close:** "Would you prefer the annual or multi-year option?"
3. **Urgency Close:** "This pricing is valid through end of quarter"
4. **Trial Close:** "If we can meet this requirement, are you ready to move forward?"

**Exit Criteria:**
- Signed contract
- Payment received or PO issued
- Kickoff meeting scheduled

#### Stage 6: Onboarding (Days 46-60)
**Activities:**
- Project kickoff meeting
- Technical setup
- Training delivery
- Success metrics baseline

**Onboarding Checklist:**
- [ ] Kickoff meeting completed
- [ ] Technical requirements confirmed
- [ ] User accounts created
- [ ] Training scheduled
- [ ] Success metrics defined
- [ ] Check-in meetings scheduled
