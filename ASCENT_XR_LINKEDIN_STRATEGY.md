## BEST PRACTICES

1. **Consistency > Frequency:** Better to post 3 high-quality posts weekly than 7 mediocre ones
2. **Video Content:** Posts with video get 5x more engagement
3. **Timing:** Post when your audience is most active (8-9 AM, 11 AM-1 PM, 3-4 PM EST)
4. **Hashtags:** Use 3-5 relevant hashtags, mix popular and niche
5. **Call to Action:** Every post should have a clear next step
6. **Personalization:** Tag relevant people/organizations when appropriate
7. **Value First:** 80% value, 20% promotion
