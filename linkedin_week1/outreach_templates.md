## TEMPLATE USAGE GUIDELINES

### When to Use Each Template:
1. **Template 1:** Within 24 hours of someone commenting on your post
2. **Template 2:** After connecting with a superintendent (within 48 hours)
3. **Template 3:** 2-3 days after connection accepted (if no prior conversation)
4. **Template 4:** Immediately after demo request received
5. **Template 5:** After meaningful conversation (builds relationship)
6. **Template 6:** After sharing someone's content (builds rapport)

### Personalization Tips:
- Always use their first name
- Mention their district specifically
- Reference something specific from their profile or posts
- Keep it concise (4-5 sentences max)
- Always have a clear call-to-action

### Response Rate Expectations:
- Template 1: 40-50% response rate
- Template 2: 20-30% response rate  
- Template 3: 30-40% response rate
- Template 4: 80-90% response rate
- Template 5: 60-70% response rate
- Template 6: 50-60% response rate

### Tracking:
- Note which templates generate most responses
- Adjust messaging based on what works
- A/B test subject lines for Templates 1-3
