### Credit Calculation Options:

```
Current Year QREs: $100,000
Base Amount (fixed % of revenue): $50,000
Excess: $50,000

```

- Must be <5 years old AND <$5M revenue
- Credit up to $250K against payroll taxes
- Use if no income tax liability

## üõí SALES TAX (SAAS) QUICK GUIDE

### States Where SaaS is Taxable:

| State | Taxable | Rate | Nexus Threshold |
|-------|---------|------|-----------------|
| **Indiana** | YES | 7% | $100K sales OR 200 transactions |
| **Ohio** | YES | 5.75% | $100K OR 200 transactions |
| **Illinois** | YES | 6.25% + local | $100K |
| **Michigan** | YES | 6% | $100K |
| **California** | NO | N/A | N/A |
| **New York** | YES | 4% + local | $500K AND 100 transactions |
| **Texas** | YES | 6.25% | $500K |

### What Triggers Nexus:
- **Economic Nexus:** Sales exceed state threshold
- **Physical Presence:** Office, employees, inventory
- **Affiliate Nexus:** Related companies in state
- **Click-Through:** Referrals from state residents

### Compliance Action Plan:
1. **Track sales monthly by state** (use spreadsheet)
2. **Register 30 days BEFORE crossing threshold**
3. **Collect tax on all sales once registered**
4. **File returns monthly or quarterly**
5. **Use Avalara or TaxJar for automation**

### Indiana Sales Tax Specifics:
- **Rate:** 7% (state only, no local)
- **Filing:** Monthly (due 20th) or quarterly
- **Registration:** INTIME.in.gov
- **SaaS Status:** Taxable as pre-written software
