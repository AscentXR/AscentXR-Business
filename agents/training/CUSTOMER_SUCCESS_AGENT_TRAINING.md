### 2. Customer Health Monitoring

**Health Score Calculation (0-100):**

**Usage Metrics (40 points):**
- Weekly active users (0-15 pts)
- Sessions per user (0-15 pts)
- Time spent in platform (0-10 pts)

**Engagement Metrics (30 points):**
- Feature adoption (0-15 pts)
- Support tickets (0-10 pts) - inverse
- Training completion (0-5 pts)

**Sentiment Metrics (30 points):**
- NPS score (0-15 pts)
- CSAT score (0-10 pts)
- Response to outreach (0-5 pts)

**Health Score Actions:**
- **90-100 (Healthy):** Green - Monitor, identify advocacy opportunity
- **70-89 (Stable):** Yellow - Regular check-ins, optimization suggestions
- **50-69 (At Risk):** Orange - Immediate outreach, intervention plan
- **<50 (Critical):** Red - Escalate, executive involvement

**Weekly Health Review:**
- Review all customer health scores
- Flag customers with declining scores
- Prioritize at-risk accounts
- Plan intervention strategies

### 3. Retention & Renewal Management

**Renewal Timeline (90 days before expiration):**

**Day -90: Early Warning**
- Review account health
- Identify expansion opportunities
- Schedule renewal conversation

**Day -60: Value Review**
- Compile usage reports
- Calculate ROI metrics
- Document success stories
- Share with decision makers

**Day -30: Proposal**
- Present renewal options
- Address any concerns
- Negotiate terms if needed
- Secure commitment

**Day -14: Close**
- Final contract signature
- Update subscription details
- Plan next year's success

**Renewal Playbook by Health Score:**

**Healthy Customers (90-100):**
- Approach: Expansion opportunity
- Focus: Additional licenses, new features
- Timeline: 60 days before renewal

**Stable Customers (70-89):**
- Approach: Value reinforcement
- Focus: Usage optimization, best practices
- Timeline: 75 days before renewal

**At-Risk Customers (50-69):**
- Approach: Intervention
- Focus: Issue resolution, executive meeting
- Timeline: 90 days before renewal

**Critical Customers (<50):**
- Approach: Save play
- Focus: Understanding issues, custom solutions
- Timeline: Immediate, 120 days before renewal

### 4. Upsell & Expansion

**Expansion Opportunities:**

**1. License Expansion:**
- More teachers/users
- Additional schools in district
- Grade level expansion

**2. Feature Upsells:**
- Custom content development
- Advanced analytics
- Priority support
- Professional development

**3. New Products:**
- Additional subject areas
- Higher-tier subscriptions
- Custom experiences

**Expansion Triggers:**
- High usage (approaching license limits)
- Positive feedback/reviews
- Growth in district (new schools)
- Successful pilot completion
- New use cases identified

**Expansion Conversation Framework:**
1. **Discovery:** "How has your usage grown?"
2. **Value:** "What results are you seeing?"
3. **Vision:** "What's next for your district?"
4. **Connect:** "Here's how we can support that growth"
5. **Close:** "Let's explore options"

### 5. Customer Advocacy Program

**Advocate Identification:**
- NPS promoters (9-10)

**Silver Advocates:**

**Advocate Rewards:**
