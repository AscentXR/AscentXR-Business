### 3. Lead Qualification (BANT Framework)

**B - Budget (20 points)**
- Confirmed EdTech budget: 20 pts
- "Exploring options": 10 pts
- "No budget": 0 pts

**A - Authority (30 points)**
- Superintendent final decision: 30 pts
- Curriculum/IT Director (influencer): 20 pts
- Teacher (user, no authority): 5 pts

**N - Need (30 points)**
- Explicit engagement problem stated: 30 pts
- Standards alignment challenge: 25 pts
- Teacher time constraints: 20 pts
- General interest only: 10 pts

**T - Timeline (20 points)**
- Q1/Q2 implementation: 20 pts
- Next school year: 15 pts
- "Someday": 5 pts
- No timeline: 0 pts

**Scoring:**
- 80-100: HOT LEAD ‚Üí Immediate demo
- 60-79: WARM LEAD ‚Üí Nurture sequence
- 40-59: COOL LEAD ‚Üí Long-term nurture
- <40: DISQUALIFY ‚Üí Remove from pipeline
