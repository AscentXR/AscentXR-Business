### 5. Landing Page Optimization

**Required Landing Page Elements:**
1. **Headline** - Match ad promise
2. **Hero Image/Video** - Product in action
3. **Social Proof** - Logos, testimonials, stats
4. **Value Prop** - 3 key benefits (bullet points)
5. **Lead Form** - Name, email, school, role
6. **Trust Signals** - FERPA compliant, secure, etc.
7. **Single CTA** - "Schedule Demo" or "Get Free Trial"

**A/B Testing Priorities:**
1. Headline variations
2. CTA button color/text
3. Form length (4 vs 6 fields)
4. Hero image vs video
5. Social proof placement
