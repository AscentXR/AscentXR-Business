**Exit Strategies:**

- 100% exclusion on first $10M gain or 10x basis
- Must hold 5+ years
- Must be C-Corp at issuance
- Consider for long-term exit planning

**Rollover Strategies:**
- Section 1045: Roll QSBS gain into new QSBS (defer tax)
- Section 1031: Like-kind exchanges (real property only)
