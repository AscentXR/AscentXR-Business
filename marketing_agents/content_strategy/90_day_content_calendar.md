# 90-Day Content Calendar
## Ascent XR Marketing Content Strategy

**Created:** February 1, 2026  
**Coverage:** February 1 - April 30, 2026  
**Goal:** Establish consistent content pipeline across all channels to drive leads and brand awareness

## Content Strategy Overview

### Primary Objectives
1. **Lead Generation:** Convert educators and administrators into qualified leads
2. **Brand Authority:** Position Ascent XR as thought leader in immersive education
3. **Customer Education:** Showcase product value and implementation success
4. **Community Building:** Engage with educational community across platforms

### Content Pillars
1. **Educational Impact** (40%): Student outcomes, teacher success stories
2. **Technical Innovation** (30%): XR technology, AI integration, platform features
3. **Leadership Insights** (20%): Jim/Nick perspectives, industry trends
4. **Community Engagement** (10%): Interactive content, Q&A, polls

### Target Audience Segmentation
- **Superintendents & Administrators:** ROI focus, district-wide impact
- **Curriculum Directors:** Standards alignment, implementation ease
- **Teachers:** Classroom-ready resources, time-saving benefits
- **EdTech Community:** Innovation discussions, partnership opportunities

## Content Formats & Specifications

### Blog Posts
- **Length:** 800-1,200 words
- **Frequency:** 2 per week (Tue/Thu)
- **SEO Focus:** Long-tail educational keywords
- **CTA:** Download related resource or schedule demo

### LinkedIn Posts
- **Length:** 300-500 words (main post) + 100-200 words (comment)
- **Frequency:** 3 per week per profile (Jim, Nick, Company)
- **Format:** Problem → Solution → Result framework
- **Hashtags:** 3-5 relevant hashtags per post

### Email Newsletters
- **Frequency:** Weekly (Friday)
- **Segments:** New leads, engaged subscribers, customers
- **Content Mix:** 1 main article + 2-3 resources + 1 CTA
- **Metrics Target:** 25% open rate, 3% CTR

### Social Media Content
- **Platform-Specific Formats:**
  - **LinkedIn:** Articles, carousels, videos (1-3 min)
  - **Twitter:** Threads, polls, quick tips, news sharing
  - **YouTube:** Tutorials (3-5 min), demos (1-2 min), webinars (30-60 min)
  - **Instagram:** Reels (30-60 sec), carousels, stories

### Premium Content
- **Case Studies:** 2-3 pages, PDF format, gated content
- **Whitepapers:** 5-10 pages, research-backed, gated content
- **Webinars:** Monthly, with Q&A, promoted across channels
- **Resource Guides:** Practical tools and templates
