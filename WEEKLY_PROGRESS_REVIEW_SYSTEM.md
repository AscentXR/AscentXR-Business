## 1. WEEKLY REVIEW MEETING STRUCTURE

### Monday Morning Review (30 minutes max)
**Time:** 9:00 AM EST  
**Format:** Virtual (Zoom/Teams)  
**Attendees:** Jim, Nick, Ascent Assistant (AI)

### Agenda Template:
```
ASCENT XR WEEKLY PROGRESS REVIEW - WEEK [X]

1. QUICK WIN CELEBRATION (2 min)
   - What went well last week?
   - Any small victories to celebrate?

2. DASHBOARD REVIEW (10 min)
   - Week [X-1] task completion status
   - Revenue pipeline updates
   - Risk register review

3. KEY METRICS (5 min)
   - LinkedIn engagement (posts, connections, responses)
   - Client meetings scheduled/completed
   - Development progress (experiences adapted)
   - Prospect pipeline growth

4. BLOCKERS & CHALLENGES (5 min)
   - What's slowing us down?
   - Resources needed?
   - Decisions required?

5. WEEK [X] PRIORITIZATION (8 min)
   - Top 3 priorities for each person/team
   - Clear ownership & deadlines
   - Success criteria for each

NEXT REVIEW: [Date] at [Time]
ACTION ITEMS: [List with owners]
```

## BEST PRACTICES & TIPS

### For Effective Reviews:
1. **Stick to 30 minutes** - Respect time boundaries
2. **Come prepared** - Fill templates before meeting
3. **Focus on solutions** - Not just problems
4. **Celebrate wins** - Build momentum
5. **Be data-driven** - Use metrics, not feelings

### For Consistent Tracking:
1. **Daily 5-minute check-ins** - Keep momentum
2. **Friday afternoon updates** - Prepare for Monday
3. **Use templates consistently** - Reduce cognitive load
4. **Review system monthly** - Improve what's not working

### For Accountability:
1. **Clear ownership** - One person per action item
2. **Specific due dates** - Not "sometime next week"
3. **Regular follow-ups** - Mid-week check-ins
4. **Transparent tracking** - Everyone sees progress
